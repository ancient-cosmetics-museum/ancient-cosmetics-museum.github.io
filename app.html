ArcheoCosmesis Interactive Application

The opening page of the ArcheoCosmesis app: descriptions of contents and aims.

Home page and menu: 4 sections to explore: the collection, the museums, help page and personal profile.

The collection: items are at first sorted aphabetically.

The collection: possibility of filtering results through search by age, civilization or theme.

Each type of item has its own description and options of listening to its audio descriptions, share the item on social media or saving it as a favorite item.

List of national archeological museums, sorted by distance from the geo position of the user.

Social network function of the app: the user can create a personal profile with a small biography or presentation, adding photograps of their favorite items took during visits in real tangible museums.

  • Created by Alessia Cioffi, Sara Coppini and Francesca Genovese for the final project of the course Museology, museography and virtual enviroment within the master degree course Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge at the University of Bologna.
  • The photographs of ArcheoCosmesis virtual museum belong to a number of national and international museums which kindly give permission to use them. Notably, we are much obliged to the British Museum, Museo Egizio of Turin, and Musei Reali of Turin.

Green Malachite

Upper eyelids were painted black and lower ones were colored green. Green was created by using malachite (a basic copper carbonate: Cu2CO3(OH)2).

Black Galena

Egyptians used to paint the upper eyelids with black. The blackpigment used with this purpose came from Galena. Galena eye paint was widely applied in Ancient Egypt to produce the so called KOHL.

Lead

Eye-paints were commonly created from using natural lead-based compounds to extract the pigments (Martinetto et al., 2001). Red lead in particular, even if dangerous for health, was widely used to paint eyes (eyelid).

Red Ocher

The red was created by an earthy pigment called ochre, which was most likely mixed with a vegetable oil, or another form of grease to get the paste-like consistency for the lips (Hayes, 1990)
